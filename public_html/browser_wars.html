The Dot Com Bubble

  • Today the internet is not complete without one ability to buy anything and everything on it. We can find almost every product sold online, and it is easy to forget that it was not always the case.

  • Amazon was launched in the summer of 1995 by Jeff Bezos, it was started thinking in a systematic way about webs unique properties, and what kinds of stuff it would be ideal for selling.

  • Wall street well and truly had its eye on Amazon due to its success, Amazon stock price doubled, not in a course of a year but within a few weeks. This led to a buying frenzy and was the start of the internet bubble where millions of Americans were suddenly buying stocks for anything with a dot com.
